Frozen Pizza

Yout don't always need to go to a pizzeria to have great pizza!.

Pick up a frozen pizza from the frozen aisle at your local grocer, bake it in your oven, and enjoy an amazing pizza from the comfort of your home at a fraction of the price.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Pizza of your choice
  • Bonus: Additional veggies/toppings

Steps

  1. Keep your pizza frozen until ready to cook
  2. Read the package of your pizza and preheat the oven to the specified temp
  3. Take the pizza from the freezer and remove the packaging, including the plastic film
  4. Bonus: Add toppings/veggies to your frozen pizza for added flavor. Careful with water-heavy veggies.
  5. Place the pizza in the oven and set the timer for the specified time
  6. Remove from oven (careful, it's hot!)
  7. Slice with a pizza cutter or knife and enjoy!
