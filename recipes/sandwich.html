Sandwich

As simple as they are, sandwiches are some of the best meals available.

With just a few ingredients, you can be in bliss with a nice sandwich. Bonus points if you have a press.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Sliced Meat
  • Sliced Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Spread mayonnaise on both sides of bread
  2. Add 4 pieces of slices meat to the bread
  3. Add 1 piece of sliced cheese on top of the meat
  4. Peel and chop the lettuce until it is about the size of the bread slices
  5. Add lettuce on top of the cheese
  6. Slice the tomato
  7. Add 2-3 tomato slices on top of the lettuce
  8. Add the other slice of bread on top
  9. Bonus: Press your sandwich using a sandwich press
  10. Enjoy!
