Bowl of Cereal

Cereal has dated back thousands of years and is still very popular today. It can be as simple as pouring milk over bite-size pieces of fried flour (bran), or more complex with different seeds, granola, fruit and more!

This recipe has an easy-to-make cereal mix that is sure to taste good and provide plenty of vitamins to get your day going!

Ingredients

  • Bowl
  • Spoon
  • Box of Bran Flakes
  • Strawberries
  • Blueberries
  • Granola Clusters
  • Milk (Alternative milks such as almond work as well)

Steps

  1. Cut strawberries into small bites
  2. Add Bran Flakes to bowl
  3. Add strawberries to bowl
  4. Add blueberries to bowl
  5. Add granola clusters to bowl
  6. Add milk to bowl
  7. Use your spoon to eat!
